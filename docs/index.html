    About Us

    We are a dedicated and passionate group of fully-qualified and compliant Trainers and Assessors who have more than ten (10) years Industry experience. Some of our trainers currently work as Registered Nurses, Aged Care Facility Managers and Directors of Nursing. This in turn provides a wealth of professional experience and as such, an extra dimension to our training delivery – assisting our students to better achieve their professional goals and employment opportunities/ outcomes.

    We understand how busy life is and its challenges: work, family, the constant need to remain ‘current’, ‘upskill’ and improve professionally with relevant qualifications to name a few. Flexibility is the key to a successful study/life balance. At Australia Wide Training Institute, we pride ourselves on promoting a flexible learning environment for our students. This is achieved through a combination of face-to-face and distance/online learning.

    Our Mission

    The Institution prepares students to understand, contribute to, and succeed in a rapidly changing society, thus making the world a better and more just place.

    Our Plan

    Ensuring that our students develop both the skills that a sound education provides and the competencies essential for success and leadership in the emerging creative economy.

    Our Vision

    Creativity, innovation and Social Engagement should orient students' academic experiences to help them become critically engaged citizens, dedicated to solving problems and contributing to the public good.

    Courses

    CHC33015 - Certificate III in Individual Support

    This course reflects the role of workers in the community and/or a residential setting who follow an individualised plan to provide person-centric support to people who may require support due to ageing, disability or other illnesses. Gain skills & knowledge necessary in order to safely care for those who struggle to care for themselves.

    CHC43015 - Certificate IV in Ageing Support

    This is the next step for an aged care worker, providing more focus on leadership, individual responsibility & collaboration with other stakeholders in the Aged Care Sector.

    BSB30115 - Certificate III in Business

    An Industry-flexible course, providing basic yet broad knowledge of how a business operates & how to work as part of a business team.

    HLTAID003 Provide First Aid

    We train you to provide First Aid response including Basic Life Support and CPR to a casualty in a range of situations, including community, healthcare & workplace settings.
